Meet the mascot

Dr. Blue

Dr. Blue has seen it all. The scope that was already broken. The charge nurse who disappeared at exactly the wrong moment. The consult that took four hours to say what everyone already knew.

He doesn't sugarcoat. He doesn't motivate. He just stands there with that look — the one that says "I told you this would happen."

He is every healthcare worker who has ever survived another shift by finding the humor in the chaos.

DeadpanSeen everythingAnti-adminProtective of staffNever breaksAlways right
"I found it this way. It was already broken when I picked it up."
His three modes

One face. Infinite situations.

Mode 01 — Default
The Judge

Phone in hand. Eyebrows furrowed. Scrolling through something he disapproves of. Use for admin decisions, bad policies, and anything that should have been obvious.

Mode 02 — Situational
The Witness

He's watching something unfold. He knew it was going to happen. He's not intervening. Use for before/after content and predictable disasters.

Mode 03 — Establishing
In His World

Dr. Blue in the OR with Stat Society signage. Use for brand intro posts, milestones, and X Spaces promos.

The golden rule

Dr. Blue never smiles. The face never changes. The situations do. That contrast is the entire joke.
